Instructions

This quiz is about the capitals of many countries in the world. Once you click on Let's Play you will be asked to provide a username, so your scored can be saved and showed if you are in the highest 3 scores. Then you will be presented with 10 randomly generated multiple choice questions. Once you select one a Next button will appear to continue to the next question. If you click the correct answer the background will become green otherwise will become red and the correct answer will be shown. At the end of the quiz your score will be shown and if you made it to the 3 highest scores, it will be shown on the highscores list.

During the quiz, at the two bottom corners you can see your current score and how many questions you have answered so far. Each correct question will give 5 points.

Before and after the quiz, you can make menu selections by pressing the in the top right corner and if you want to return to the starting page you can press the in the top left corner.

Through the menu selections you can select the Contact page where you can send us any message you want.
